ISK, The Native Token of ISKRA

ISK is a native token of ISKRA Platform and is used in all services in the ecosystem. The key roles of ISK can be divided into three component as follows.

  • Exclusive Currency for Pioneer NFT

    ISK is used as exclusive currency for purchasing Pioneer NFT. Except the initial 7,000 units, You can only buy it with ISK.

  • Governance Token

    ISK is a governance token that can be used for governance proposals and voting.

  • Key Currency

    As the Key Currency, ISK is used for all purpose in the ISKRA ecosystem Such as means of exchange with other assets, transaction fee and various services to be added.

How Can I Get ISK?

There are few ways to get ISK But Today, We will introduce the two ways to obtain ISK.

  • Pioneer NFT

    40% of the total supply of ISK will be distributed to Pioneer NFT holders over 10 years. This will be the surest way to get the largest amount of ISK. The most important thing you must know is that the sooner you become a Pioneer, the more ISK you can receive. Don't forget to stake the Pioneer NFT you purchased.

  • ISKRA Dex or other Exchanges

    The simplest way to get ISK is to just buy it. ISKRA will list ISK on major exchanges as well as ISKRA DEX, and you can buy ISK at market prices.
